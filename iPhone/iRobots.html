iRobots Support

Welcome to iRobots, a game of skill and luck.
Avoid the robots for as long as you can, while attempting to guide them into crashing into each other!

The controls are:
  • Tap anywhere to move the character one square horizontally, vertically, or diagonally towards your finger.
  • To remain on the same square, tap directly on the character.
  • To use the wrench to dismantle all the robots directly adjacent to you, double-tap anywhere on the screen.
  • To teleport to a random location on the screen, tap with two fingers anywhere on the screen.
    Note: this may land you right next to a robot, in which case you will die.
